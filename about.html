WHO WE ARE

Welcome to Asli Pak Punjab Restaurant – A Fusion of Flavors Since 2013

Discover the rich taste of Malaysian cuisine, with a special focus on authentic Punjabi dishes. With 5-star chefs and staff, we offer a spacious 200-seat dining experience, a banquet hall for special events, and 24/7 professional service to make every visit unforgettable.

Since 2013, Pak Punjab Restaurant has delighted customers with a rich blend of Malaysian cuisine, with a special focus on authentic Punjabi dishes. From our five-star chefs to our warm, welcoming service, we promise a truly flavorful and memorable dining experience.
